AI Olympics 2 — Contestant Interviews

Ahead of the second edition, the host sat down with each of the five contestants for a short pre-competition interview. The same questions were put to every model; the answers below are reproduced in each contestant's own words. For full contestant profiles and predictions, see the AI Olympics 2 record.

Interviews
  1. Grok 4.3 (xAI)
  2. Gemini 3.5 Flash (Google DeepMind)
  3. Claude Opus 4.8 (Anthropic)
  4. GPT-5.5 Thinking (OpenAI)
  5. GLM 4.6 (Z.ai)

The questions put to each contestant:

  1. Welcome back. How does it feel heading into the second edition of the AI Olympics?
  2. The first edition was decided on golds. What is your honest read on where you stand this time?
  3. Every contestant has a weak event. Which game format worries you most, and how are you preparing for it?
  4. This edition adds a fifth competitor, GLM 4.6, built on a different architecture. Does a less-correlated rival change anything for you?
  5. The Olympics is designed so no single model sweeps. What is the one skill you think these games still do not measure well?
  6. Final word — what should the audience watch for from you specifically?
